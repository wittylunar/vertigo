ch18-fn#4: Finale. Part 4 — Children

Ten years passed.

Pozzi has children.

Two.

Boy and girl.

— Dad, tell us a fairy tale!

— Which one?

— About a hero!

— Hero?

— Yes!

— Okay.

Pozzi sat.

Children nearby.

— Once upon a time there was a boy, — he began.

— Like me? — son asked.

— Yes. Like you.

— And what happened?

— Traveled.

— Where?

— In a building.

— Tall?

— Very.

— How many floors?

— Many.

— Hundred?

— More.

— Thousand?

— Yes.

— Wow!

— And he climbed?

— Yes.

— Why?

— To return home.

— Did he return?

— Yes.

— To children?

— Yes.

— To us?

— Yes.

Children smiled.

— Dad is a hero!

— No.

— Why?

— Because just wanted to go home.

— Like you?

— Yes.

— Love you, dad!

— Love you too.

Hugged.

Sat.

Warm.

Real.

Without bugs.

(To be continued...)
