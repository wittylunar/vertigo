ch16-sh#4: Choice

Pozzi opened the door.

Round room behind it.

With windows.

Worlds in windows.

Miami. Arctic. Iraq. Cyberpunk. Garden. Wasteland.

— What is this? — he asked.

— Room of Choice, — Luna said.

— Which choice?

— Where to live.

— Isn't there only one option?

— No.

— How many?

— Many.

— Show me.

Luna waved.

Windows lit up.

— Miami — home. But boring.

— Arctic — adventure. But cold.

— Iraq — war. But there's purpose.

— Cyberpunk — technology. But no soul.

— Garden — peace. But no challenge.

— What to choose?

— What you want.

— What if I don't know?

— Think.

— How long?

— Forever.

— What?!

— Time doesn't work here.

— How to leave?

— Choose.

— What if I don't choose?

— You'll stay.

— Forever?

— Yes.

Pozzi looked at windows.

— I want...
