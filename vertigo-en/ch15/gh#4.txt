ch15-gh#4: Mountain

Level 1600 — mountain. High, snowy, inaccessible.

— How to climb? — Pozzi asked.

— With legs.

— Long?

— Yes.

— How much?

— Depends.

— On what?

— On strength.

— Mine?

— Yes.

— Enough?

— We'll check.

Pozzi started climbing.

Step. Slippery.

Step. Wind.

Step. Cold.

— Why so hard?

— Because mountain tests.

— What?

— Desire.

— Which?

— Home.

— Strong?

— Check.

— How?

— Walk.

Pozzi walked.

Higher.

Higher.

— How much more?

— Almost.

— How do you know?

— I see.

— What?

— The peak.

— Where?

— Ahead.

Pozzi raised his head.

Peak close.
