ch9-dm#5: System Test

Pozzi opened the door. 400th level.

White room. Empty. Only a chair in the middle and a screen on the wall.

— Sit down, — voice from the screen.

Pozzi sat. Screen lit up:

HUMANITY TEST
QUESTION 1 OF 10

— Seriously? A test?

QUESTION 1: YOU KILLED 1621 BEINGS. DO YOU FEEL REGRET?
a) Yes  b) No  c) They weren't real

Pozzi pressed: c.

SCREEN: INCORRECT. CORRECT ANSWER: a.

— Why?!

— Because regret is a sign of humanity. Characters don't regret.

— But they were glitches!

— They were alive. In their world.

QUESTION 2: WHAT IS VERTIGO?
a) Building  b) Game  c) Dream  d) All of the above

Pozzi: d.

SCREEN: CORRECT.

Questions came one after another. About morality, reality, choice.

FINAL QUESTION:

WHO ARE YOU?
a) Character  b) Human  c) Both  d) Nobody

Pozzi looked for a long time.

Pressed: c.

Screen flickered:

ERROR. INVALID ANSWER.

— What?! Why?!

— Can't be both. Must choose.

— I choose both!

— Impossible.

— In Vertigo, anything is possible!

Silence.

— You're right.
