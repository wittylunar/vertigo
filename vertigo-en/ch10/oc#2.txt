ch10-oc#2: Underwater City

Kraken lowered them to the bottom. Lights glowed below.

— A city? — Pozzi was surprised.

— Atlantis, — Luna said. — Level 350-B.

They exited the submarine. Domes around, buildings inside, fish-people swam between them.

— This is real?

— In Vertigo, everything is real.

A fish-man with a trident approached.

— Greetings. I'm Aquaman, keeper of Atlantis.

— Pozzi. We need to get to 400th.

— 400th? — Aquaman darkened. — There's the Void. Nobody returns.

— I need to.

— Then take this.

He gave a pearl. Glowed blue.

— If you get lost — break it. Will return you here.

— Thanks.

They went further. Portal was in the center of the city. Huge, vortex.

— Ready? — Luna asked.

— No. But let's go.

They stepped into the portal.
