ch18-fn#3: Finale. Part 3 — Memory

Five years passed.

Pozzi got married.

Wedding on beach.

Friends. Family. Laughter.

— Do you promise? — priest asked.

— I promise, — Pozzi said.

— To love?

— To love.

— To cherish?

— To cherish.

— To be there?

— To be.

— In sickness?

— Yes.

— In health?

— Yes.

— Until death?

— No.

— What?

— Until life.

— Life?

— Yes.

— Why?

— Because death is not the end.

— Then what?

— Beginning.

— Of what?

— New path.

— You know?

— Yes.

— From where?

— Passed.

— What?

— Everything.

Priest nodded.

— I pronounce you husband and wife.

Kissed.

Applause.

Laughter.

Happiness.

In evening Pozzi looked at stars.

— Thanks, — he said.

— To whom? — wife asked.

— Everyone.

— Who everyone?

— Those who were.

— Where?

— There.

— Where there?

— In Vertigo.

— What's that?

— Story.

— Yours?

— Yes.

— Will you tell me?

— Someday.

— Promise?

— Promise.

(To be continued...)
